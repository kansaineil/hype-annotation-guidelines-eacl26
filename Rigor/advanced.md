### **1. Value-judgement**  
- Often yes: signals sophistication, superiority, or progress (≈ *state-of-the-art, high-level*).  
- **NOT** a value-judgement when part of a fixed acronym/name (*Advanced Placement, Advanced Encryption Standard*), or used technically to mean late-stage/progressed (*advanced cancer, advanced disease*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *These efforts have resulted in an **advanced** information management system at BCM and an Integrated Library System at the TMCL.* (Value-judgement)

- *These will include traditional combination chemotherapy trials in **advanced** cancer …* (NOT HYPE — technical “late-stage”)

</details>

### **3a. Gratuitous**  
- Typically gratuitous when presented in absolute terms with nouns denoting expertise, method, or technical capacity (e.g., *training, imaging, research, method, technology, technique, data, microscopy, skill, development, statistics, biostatistics, practice, knowledge, understanding, instrumentation, analysis, tool, study, computing, modeling, approach, topic, sequencing, algorithm, software, feature, capability, methodology, information*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *The core of the **advanced** training program consists of …* (Gratuitous)

- *These **advanced** research students will have a 2-year intensive research experience …* (Gratuitous)

- *…by the application of newly developed genetic tools and **advanced** technology.* (Gratuitous)

</details>

---

### **3b. Amplified**  
- Often amplified: *the most, greatly, highly, very, extremely, increasingly, well, far, quite, particularly, fundamentally*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *We will use highly **advanced** imaging techniques to …* (Amplified)

</details>

---

### **3c. Broader context**  
- Often stacked with other evaluative terms (e.g., *novel, innovative, unique, cutting-edge (implicit), large* (when evaluative), *current* (when evaluative), *emerging, invaluable*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *First, we will support the development and application of **advanced** and innovative statistical methodologies …* (Stacking/context)

</details>
