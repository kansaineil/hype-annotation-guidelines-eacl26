### **1. Value-judgement**  
Usually yes (evaluates complexity/advancement/refinement).  
NOT value-judgement when referring to intrinsic mechanistic complexity of a biological/physical system or process, rather than a claim of quality.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Using such an advanced and **sophisticated** device currently available in the market …* (Value-judgement, claim of quality)

- *…yet complex enough to have **sophisticated** optomotor behaviors.* (NOT HYPE — intrinsic complexity, rather than a claim of quality)

</details>

### **3. Gratuitous**  
Often gratuitous with abstract nouns (e.g., *understanding, knowledge, information, data, research, study, analysis, approach, model, measure(s), methodology, strategy, insight, level, state, set*).  
Sometimes gratuitous with capacity nouns (e.g., *tool, technique, method(s), technology/technologies, instrumentation, instrument, equipment, software, algorithm, platform*).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *This will serve as a foundation for a **sophisticated** understanding of the pathophysiology of human growth and development …*

</details>

---

### **4. Amplified**  
Sometimes amplified: *highly, increasingly, the most, very, extremely, clinically, remarkably, clearly, extraordinarily, surprisingly*

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *…provides highly **sophisticated** CRISPR Cas9 mediated genome editing in embryos and in cells …*

</details>

---

### **5. Coordinated**  
Often coordinated/stacked with other evaluative adjectives.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Using such an advanced and **sophisticated** device currently available in the market …*

</details>

---

### **6. Broader context**  
When ambiguous, consider whether surrounding context contributes to or detracts from overall promotional force.

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *Thus, we must use more **sophisticated** approaches to analyze direct interactions between the steroid receptors and associated cochaperones.*

</details>
