### **1. Value-judgement**  
Mixed.

- **NOT HYPE** when used as a numbering device (time/sequence/ordering).
- **Potential HYPE** when used as a **priority claim** (“the first to …”, “first evidence-based …”).

<details markdown="1">
<summary><em>Examples</em></summary>

- *During the **first** weeks after the onset of stroke, the injured brain undergoes several neural mechanisms …* (NOT HYPE)

- *The proposed study will be the **first** to measure the impact of these policies …* (Potential HYPE)

</details>
